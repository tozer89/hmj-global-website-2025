Who We Are

HMJ-Global is a specialist talent partner for mission-critical environments. We support data centre construction, commissioning, substations & power, and pharmaceutical manufacturing & cleanroom projects. Our model is built on pace, precision and compliance: rapid shortlists, rigorous vetting, and smooth cross-border mobilisation including payroll, right-to-work checks and H&S onboarding. We partner with tier-one contractors, systems integrators and operators to de-risk delivery and keep programmes on track.

Our Hiring Process

1. Brief & Success Profile

We capture scope, outcomes and culture, then define a success profile with must-have competencies and compliance needs.

2. Targeted Search

Proactive outreach into our DC & Life-Sciences networks, referrals and project alumni. Diversity and location filters built in.

3. Shortlist in 48 Hours

3–5 verified profiles with availability, day rates/salary expectations, location, and compliance status.

4. Interviews & Technical Checks

Structured interviews, reference checks, and (where required) technical assessments. Feedback loop to refine quickly.

5. Mobilisation

Offers, contracts, site inductions, PPE, right-to-work and payroll. We align start dates to programme critical path.
