Electrician — Data Centre Construction Project

Slough, UK Contract (CIS) Data Centre Construction
Gold Card 18th Edition 11-hour paid days ASAP start

Overview

We are recruiting experienced Gold Card Electricians to support a major data centre construction project in Slough. This is an excellent opportunity to work on a large-scale, technically demanding project within a highly regulated and safety-driven environment. Data centre experience is preferred, but strong commercial or industrial backgrounds will also be considered.

Key Responsibilities

  • Install electrical systems on a live data centre construction site.
  • Cable pulling, containment (tray, basket, trunking, conduit) and wiring installations.
  • Terminate power and distribution systems.
  • Work from electrical drawings, schematics, and method statements.
  • Ensure all work complies with project QA/QC standards.
  • Maintain high health & safety standards at all times.
  • Collaborate with supervisors and other site trades.
  • Report progress, issues, or defects to site management.

Required Experience & Qualifications

  • Gold JIB/ECS Card (essential).
  • NVQ Level 3 Electrical Installation or equivalent.
  • 18th Edition (BS7671).
  • Experience on large commercial, industrial, or data centre projects.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Strong attention to detail and safety compliance.
  • IPAF.

Desirable (Not Essential)

  • Previous data centre or mission-critical project experience.
  • Experience working long-hour shift patterns.

  • Hours: 11 hours paid per day.
