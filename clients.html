WHY HMJ

Data Centre & Infrastructure Recruitment You Can Rely On

HMJ-Global delivers specialist permanent, contract and project-team hires for data centres, pharmaceutical cleanrooms and other live environments. Our focus is pace with precision: tightly-curated shortlists, robust verification and smooth cross-border mobilisation. We help clients control risk and maintain programme velocity, while ensuring contractors are paid fairly and supported well.

HOW WE DELIVER – SERVICES

Permanent Search

Exec to site-level with market mapping, competency screening and structured interviews. Offer management and onboarding end-to-end.

Contract & Interim

Rapid deployment of vetted specialists to critical path. Weekly payroll, simple timesheets, and in-country setups where required.

Project Teams

Mobilise multi-disciplinary squads (CSA, MEP, Commissioning, CQV) under one supplier with clear KPIs and single invoicing.

Compliance & Payroll

Right-to-work, insurances, IR35 guidance, A1/PD portability, and safety onboarding. Transparent, auditable processes.

Market Intelligence

Availability heatmaps and indicative pay benchmarking to support bids and executive decision-making.

Contractor Care

Fair pay, predictable rotations where applicable, and proactive site support to maintain morale and retention.
