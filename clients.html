Delivery playbook

From brief to mobilisation in four deliberate steps

  1. Scope alignment Confirm package context, key deliverables, reporting lines, location and urgency before the search opens.
  2. Market & budget framing Pressure-test rates, availability and internal approvals so the brief is credible and easier to action.
  3. Shortlist & review Share a curated shortlist with compliance status, brief-fit commentary and practical mobilisation notes.
  4. Onboarding support Coordinate documents, payroll setup, site onboarding and contractor communication to reduce start-up friction.
