Plain-English key

What the analytics terms mean

Short explanations for the phrases used on this page, written for non-specialists.

Visit (session)

One continuous visit to the site. The same person can create more than one visit in a day.

Unique visitor

An estimated distinct person or browser seen during the selected date range.

Call to action (CTA)

A button or link asking someone to do something next, such as Apply, Contact, Call, Email, or Open a job spec.

Job spec

A job specification page or document that explains the role, requirements, and responsibilities in more detail.

Bounce rate

The share of visits where someone viewed one page only and then left without exploring further.

Landing page

The first page a visitor arrived on during a visit.

Exit page

The last page viewed before that visit ended.

Average time on page

An estimate of how long people actively spent on a page, not just how long the tab stayed open.

Top path

A common sequence of pages people move through, which helps show how they navigate the site.
