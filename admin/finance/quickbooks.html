Realm / company id QuickBooks returns the realm id after a successful OAuth callback. It is not hardcoded in HMJ admin.
Connection guidance Use the production HMJ finance URL for OAuth. Avoid preview deploys for the initial QuickBooks connection unless the same callback URL has been whitelisted in Intuit.
If Intuit shows “undefined didn’t connect” That means Intuit blocked the app before the HMJ callback fired. Check the Intuit app Production keys, callback URI, and that the app is configured in the Production tab rather than Development.
Cached entities Customers, invoices, payments, bills, and purchases are cached in Supabase for finance use.
