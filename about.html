Our story

Building dependable delivery partners, one milestone at a time

We have grown alongside the rapid build-out of European digital infrastructure and regulated pharma facilities. Each step sharpened our playbook for safe, predictable mobilisation.

2019

HMJ-Global launches

Brought together data centre recruiters and finance expertise to create a boutique partner.

Embedded inside hyperscale campus expansions in Dublin and Frankfurt, delivering technical contractors under accelerated timelines.

2020

Pan-European mobilisation

Established compliant payroll and mobility frameworks across Benelux, Nordics and Iberia.

Managed visa workflows, A1 portability, insurance and H&S onboarding for cross-border commissioning teams.

2021

Life-sciences expansion

Scaled into GMP cleanrooms and utilities, adding CQV and process engineering networks.

Partnered with pharmaceutical majors on validated utilities projects, aligning hiring to qualification gates.

2024

Integrated delivery squads

Deploy cross-functional pods that align CSA, MEP, commissioning and ops talent end-to-end.

Standing up project controls, HSE and reliability teams with digital reporting, giving clients real-time visibility on programme health.
