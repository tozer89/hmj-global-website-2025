About HMJ-Global

Specialist recruitment for mission-critical data centres and pharmaceutical environments.

What We Do

HMJ-Global is a dedicated recruitment partner for data centres, pharmaceutical manufacturing and other live-environment facilities. We support programmes from early works and fit-out through commissioning, turnover and steady-state operations. Our core belief is simple: projects succeed when the right people arrive at the right time. We work to help clients control risk and whole-life costs while ensuring contractors are paid fairly and on time—creating the conditions for safe, productive sites and long-term delivery confidence.

Where We Add Value

Data centre construction, commissioning and operations; pharma cleanrooms and validated utilities.

Construction & Fit-Out CSA/MEP

Project directors, project managers, package managers, planners, site engineers, supervisors and commercial teams. We resource tightly to programme with the compliance and culture fit your sites demand.

Commissioning & IST

Electrical & mechanical commissioning managers, QA/QC, BMS/EPMS, and black-start readiness. Our networks include talent that has delivered hyperscale and enterprise data halls across Europe.

Security & Cabling

Access control, CCTV and structured cabling for brownfield/greenfield campuses—project leads, installers, testers and commissioning engineers with live-environment experience.

HV/Substations & Power

SAPs, protection engineers, cable jointers and civils supervisors for grid tie-ins and on-site substations, aligned to energisation milestones and safety rules.

Pharma & Cleanrooms

E&I, CQV, process and utilities specialists supporting GMP documentation and validated handover to operations. We understand the rigor required around change control and traceability.

Critical Facilities Operations

Shift techs, facilities managers and reliability engineers to keep sites safe and available 24/7, including CMMS/PPM specialists and AP/AE personnel.

How We Work

1. Understand the Outcome

We translate scope into success criteria—programme dates, compliance needs and site culture—so every hire supports delivery.

2. Targeted Search

We use curated DC/Life-Sciences networks, referrals and alumni. Shortlists are lean, relevant and available.

3. Verification & Fit

Competency checks, references, right-to-work and H&S onboarding. We ensure fair pay and transparent expectations for contractors.

4. Mobilise & Support

Offers, contracts, site inductions and payroll. We keep communication tight so starts are clean and teams stay productive.
